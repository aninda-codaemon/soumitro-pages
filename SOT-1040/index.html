About the Woodcliff Lake, NJ

Woodcliff Lake is a borough in Bergen County, New Jersey, United States. Most of the borough mandates a minimum lot size of 22,500 square feet (2,090 m2) for single-family homes, with portion on the borough east zoned for 15,000 square feet (1,400 m2) and portion of the borough's northwest and southwest zoned for 30,000 square feet (2,800 m2) lots.
