Why NeighborWho?

NeighborWho’s Mission is to help people discover, understand and use public data in their everyday lives.

At NeighborWho, our overriding goal is a simple one: we want to provide you with access to public information that was formerly only available to big companies and people with deep pockets in a way that is easy, affordable, and fast. Founded in 2007, we're a talented and passionate team and one of the fastest growing companies in New York City.
